## üë∑ For All Staff Members

### Viewing Your Roster
- After logging in, you'll see the **Weekly Roster** tab
- Switch between **"This Week"** and **"Next Week"** using the buttons at the top
- Find your name in the left column
- Your shifts are shown in colored boxes with:
  - Start time - End time
  - Break times (if any)
  - Notes about the shift

### Clock In / Clock Out
1. Click on the **"Clock In"** tab
2. Click the **"Clock In"** button when you arrive at work
3. The screen will show your clock-in time
4. Click **"Clock Out"** when you finish your shift
5. Your hours will be recorded automatically

### Viewing Your Work Hours
1. Go to the **"Payroll"** tab
2. Select the date range you want to view
3. Find your name in the list
4. You'll see:
   - Total hours worked
   - Individual clock-in/clock-out records
   - Breakdown by day

## üë®‚Äçüíº For Managers Only

### Adding/Editing Shifts
1. Go to the **"Roster"** tab
2. **To add a shift:**
   - Click on any empty cell (day + employee)
   - Fill in the shift details:
     - Start time and End time
     - Break start and End (optional)
     - Notes (e.g., "Kitchen duty")
     - Select employee(s) for this shift
   - Click **"Save Shift"**

3. **To edit a shift:**
   - Click on an existing shift (colored box)
   - Make your changes
   - Click **"Save Shift"**

4. **To delete a shift:**
   - Click on the shift
   - Click **"Delete Shift"**
   - Confirm the deletion

### Adding Recurring Shifts
1. When creating a new shift, check **"Recurring Shift"**
2. Select which days to repeat the shift
3. Click **"Save Shift"**
4. The same shift will be added to all selected days

### Copying Roster to Next Week
1. Make sure you're viewing **"This Week"**
2. Click **"Copy to Next Week"** button
3. Confirm the action
4. Current week's entire roster will be copied to next week

### Sharing Roster via WhatsApp
1. Click the **"Share via WhatsApp"** button
2. A PDF will be downloaded to your device
3. WhatsApp will open automatically
4. Attach the downloaded PDF and send to your team group

### Managing Employees
1. Go to the **"Settings"** tab
2. Click **"Employees"** section
3. **To add a new employee:**
   - Click **"Add Employee"**
   - Enter name, role, and PIN
   - Click **"Save"**

4. **To edit an employee:**
   - Click the edit icon (pencil) next to their name
   - Update details
   - Click **"Save"**

5. **To change someone's PIN:**
   - Edit the employee
   - Change the PIN field
   - Click **"Save"**

### Editing Time Logs
1. Go to **"Payroll"** tab
2. Click the edit icon next to any time log entry
3. Adjust clock-in or clock-out times if needed
4. Click **"Save"**

### Backing Up Data
1. Go to **"Settings"** tab
2. Click **"Export Data"**
3. A JSON file will download - **save this file safely**
4. You can restore this data later using "Import Data"

### Restoring Data from Backup
1. Go to **"Settings"** tab
2. Click **"Import Data"**
3. Select your backup JSON file
4. All data will be restored

## üÜò Troubleshooting

**Can't log in?**
- Check you've selected the correct name
- Make sure you're entering the right PIN (default is `1234`)
- Ask your manager to verify your PIN

**Don't see your shifts?**
- Make sure you're looking at the correct week (This Week vs Next Week)
- Ask your manager if shifts have been scheduled yet

**Clock In button not working?**
- Check your internet connection
- Try refreshing the page
- Contact your manager if the problem continues

**Data not saving?**
- Check your internet connection
- Don't close the browser immediately after making changes
- If data keeps disappearing, notify your manager immediately

**Forgot your PIN?**
- Ask your manager to check/reset your PIN in Settings
